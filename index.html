lp: running the tao te ching - 2

When runners see some runs as beautiful
other runs become ugly
When runners see some runs as good
other runs become bad

Running and non-running create each other
Difficult and easy runs support each other
Long and short runs define each other
A runner’s highs and lows depend on each other
The time before and after running follow each other

Therefore the Master
runs without doing anything
and instructs without saying anything
Things arise and she lets them come
Things disappear and she lets them go
She has ability without possessing it
runs without expectation

When her run is finished, she forgets it
that is why it lasts forever
